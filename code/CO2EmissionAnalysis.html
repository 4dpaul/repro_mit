Introduction

This is a mini-report to analyze and visualize the EDGAR CO2 emissions 1970-2016 dataset. The dataset is downloaded from the GitHub repository at https://github.com/openclimatedata/edgar-co2-emissions on January 23, 2020.

The dataset was originally reported in Crippa, M., Oreggioni, G., Guizzardi, D., Muntean, M., Schaaf, E., Lo Vullo, E., Solazzo, E., Monforti-Ferrario, F., Olivier, J.G.J., Vignati, E., Fossil CO2 and GHG emissions of all world countries - 2019 Report, EUR 29849 EN, Publications Office of the European Union, Luxembourg, 2019, ISBN 978-92-76-11100-9, https://doi.org/10.2760/687800, JRC117610.

More about the dataset and similar climate datasets can be found at https://openclimatedata.net/

It contains columns Code, Name, Sector, Year, Emissions. They are the country code, country Name, cateogrical sectors, year of CO2 emission was calculated, and the emission in kilotons (kt). The categorical sectors include Buildings, Other industrial combustion, Other sectors, Power Industry, Transport.

The unit kilotons for the Emission column was not noted in the original dataset. We added the unit to the column name as Emissions_kt and saved the new dataset under the data_derived subfolder.
